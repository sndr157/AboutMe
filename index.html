In this role, nothing was simply given but earned through hard work and dedication. It was like navigating a ship through the undefined waters of finance,compliance and lending

With each crest and trough, I found myself growing, transitioning into an independent Mortgage Broker at Century 21 University Learning (July 1999 - June 2000).
